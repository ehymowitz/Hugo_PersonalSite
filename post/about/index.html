About Me

Hi! Thanks for visiting my website! This is still VERY much a work in progress.

It is also where I get to write and post about things that I make and work on. So if you’re interested in learning about me, this is the place for that.

I’m originally from Seattle, Washington but I’ve been living in Montreal for the last 8 years (since September 2012). Before I came here, I spent a gap year living in Israel. While studying engineering at McGill I developed an appreciation for all STEM related topics, and also realized my passion for playing music. I picked up the bass guitar and never really put it down.

I began playing music with a few friends from school in a pop/punk band called Free Hats, you can find a few of our songs on Spotify! Since then I’ve began playing with a bunch of great Montreal artists including Andria Piperni, Melissa Plett, and Aquarius Dreams. In addition to this I play with a number of cover bands around Montreal, and you can find me at the local jam sessions too!

Aside from music, I work at a great startup called Haply Robotics. At Haply we work in the field of haptics - robotics dealing with the re-creation of touch. Our goal is to make this technology accessible to those who wish to use and learn with it. We work across a wide range of aspects of this technology, but the core belief is that using haptics, interaction with technology takes on a whole new positive dimension.

Finally, I do a little bit of freelance work; teaching music, STEM topic tutoring, web design, and some photography too. If you’d like examples of my work or want to know anything more about me, I’d love to hear from you! You can also check out the links on the side panel of this website to see any of my social media - including my GitHub where I have a few projects.
